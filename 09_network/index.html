WEEK 9: NETWORKING / IOT


For this week, we focused on understanding how internet works and how we use it to control boards in the area.


For the assingment, I worked with LEDs and tried controlling them through the internet on Firebase. The link for the resources, I used are here. I found many of my hardships came with configuring Arduino with Firebase. I was able to get my LED light with the ESP32 - Feather but I did find it available to turn off or on in the Realtime Database on Firebase.
