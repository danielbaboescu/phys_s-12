WEEK 6: 3D Design and Printing


Part of the classwork today was to download a 3D printing software. So I first decided to try out the Prusa Slicer software. However, this didn't really work out for me, so I moved on to use the Cura software to better success. For this assignment, I chose to create a small model that could not be (easily) made by subtractive methods. I decided to create a sauce container (lid and base) by revolving a sketch on the front plane by its side to create its hollow and cylindrical like shape.
