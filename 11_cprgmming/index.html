WEEK 11: COMPUTER PROGRAMMING


This week, I knew I wanted to try out casting and modeling as a way to add to the list of things that I could put into my final project organizer.


So, I decided to try to cast two model animals that I got from Arizona. Using the instructions found on week 12, which link can be found here, I mixed formula A and B and casted my models. At first, I thought of doing a full cast of the model and then switched to a half cast to not fully submerge the model, but after a bit of internal debate, I switched back to full cast.


Overall, I did find the experience refreshing. I got to do something fun like casting and modeling. Though I do regret that I used a lot of the formula to make the casts for the models leaving barely any remains.


Besides finding new stuff to add to my organizer through casting and modeling, I also began to construct the website that would be used for the application for the Autonomous Desktop Organizer.
